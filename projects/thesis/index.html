As part of my PhD with the new IGGI DTC I am currently planning on investigating the impact of using abstracted game states on the performance of Monte Carlo Tree Search to difficult turn-based games where there are a huge number of cominations of subactions performable during a single turn (think Civilizaion or XCOM). This is an extension of my MEng Project, "Plan-Base Monte Carlo Tree Search".
